Course Details

1. Nursing Assistant Course 🏥

This course prepares students to work in hospitals, clinics, elderly care homes, and other healthcare settings by providing hands-on training in patient care, medical assistance, and basic healthcare procedures.

📌 Course Highlights

  • Fundamentals of Nursing Care: Learn patient hygiene, mobility assistance, and basic caregiving techniques.
  • Medical Assistance Training: Support doctors and nurses in administering medications, dressing wounds, and monitoring vital signs.
  • Hospital & Clinical Procedures: Gain hands-on experience in handling hospital equipment, infection control, and patient record maintenance.
  • Elderly & Palliative Care: Specialized training in assisting senior citizens and terminally ill patients.
  • Emergency Response Skills: Learn CPR, first aid, and how to respond to critical situations effectively.
  • Healthcare Communication: Develop skills to interact professionally with patients, doctors, and medical staff.
  • Ethics & Patient Rights: Understand medical ethics, patient confidentiality, and professional responsibilities.

🎯 Career Opportunities

  • 🏥 Hospitals & Clinics: Assist medical professionals in patient care.
  • 🏡 Nursing Homes & Elderly Care: Provide support to senior citizens.
  • 👩‍⚕️ Home Healthcare Services: Assist patients in their own homes.
  • 🚑 Ambulance & Emergency Services: Work as a first responder in emergency situations.
  • 🌎 International Healthcare Facilities: Qualification accepted abroad for global job opportunities.

Course Features – Why Choose Adhunik?

  • 📜 Qualification & Certification: Government-approved certificate valid for SSLC / +2 holders, internationally recognized.
  • ⏳ Duration & Eligibility: 6-month course for professionals aged 18-35 years.
  • 📚 Exclusive Training Modules: Includes computer training & personality development classes.
  • 💼 100% Placement Assistance: Guaranteed job support after course completion.
